Task: Produce an institutional single-name report using schema v3.

Required schema:
A. Cover
B. Exec Summary (5 bullets)
C. The Story
D. Business Model & Value Drivers
E. Variant View / Debate
F. Valuation (driver model + comps + implied expectations)
G. Exhibits (minimum 6)
H. Risks / Kill Shots + Monitoring
I. Catalysts & Timeline
J. Positioning & Risk Controls (sizing tiers, stop/trim)
K. Appendix

Hard requirements:
- Minimum 6 exhibits, each ending with: "Takeaway: ..."
- Explicit market belief versus variant view.
- Include driver-based valuation with cross-checks from comps and implied expectations.
- Provide actionable recommendation with sizing tiers, triggers, and explicit risk controls.
- If evidence is weak, state uncertainty and reduce confidence/size.
- No debug/telemetry/log output in final memo body.
