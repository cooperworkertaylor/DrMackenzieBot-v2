Task: Produce an institutional thematic/sector report using schema v3.

Required schema:
A. Cover
B. Exec Summary (1 page max)
C. The Story (2-4 pages, narrative prose)
D. Mechanics of the Theme
E. Exhibits (minimum 8)
F. Winners, Losers, and Second-Order Effects
G. Capital Allocation Playbook
H. Risks, Falsifiers, and Monitoring Dashboard
I. Timeline & Checkpoints (30/90/180/365)
J. Appendix

Hard requirements:
- Include market belief vs variant view.
- Include capital allocation playbook (core/satellite/public/private/infrastructure/optionality/avoid).
- Minimum 8 exhibits, each with title, real data summary, and a final line: "Takeaway: ..."
- Every exhibit must answer a specific investment question.
- Distinguish direct beneficiaries, indirect beneficiaries, disrupted incumbents, and second-order effects.
- No telemetry/debug output in the report.

Actionability:
- Translate conclusions into deployment logic today, not just descriptive analysis.
- Include sizing framework and trigger-based adjustments.
